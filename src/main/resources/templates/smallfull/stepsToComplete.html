File full accounts

Use this service to file prepared and approved full accounts.

If you can't finish your filing in one session, you can come back later to continue. You'll need to go to 'Your filings'

There are 8 steps to complete:

  1. 1Confirm your company account dates
  2. 2Directors Report (if applicable)
  3. 3Profit and loss account (if applicable)
  4. 4Balance sheet
  5. 5Balance sheet statements
  6. 6Balance sheet notes
  7. 7Check your answers
  8. 8Approval and submit
