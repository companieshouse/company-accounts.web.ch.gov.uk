File full accounts

Use this service to file prepared and approved full accounts.

If you can't finish your filing in one session, you can come back later to continue. You'll need to go to 'Your filings'

There are 5 steps to complete:

  1. 1 Balance sheet
  2. 2 Balance sheet statements
  3. 3 Balance sheet notes
  4. 4 Check your answers
  5. 5 Approve and submit
