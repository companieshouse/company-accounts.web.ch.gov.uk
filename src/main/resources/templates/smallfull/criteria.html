Criteria

Check your prepared accounts to see if you meet the criteria.

You can file full accounts here if:

  • you don't need to include a directors' report or profit and loss account
  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to
  • your balance sheet doesn’t have 'fixed asset investments', 'current asset investments', 'revaluation reserve', or 'intangible assets'
  • you only need to add notes (if applicable) for 'accounting policies', 'tangible assets', 'stocks', 'debtors', 'creditors - amounts falling due within one year', or 'creditors - amounts falling due after one year'

Your full accounts must meet at least 2 of the following:

  • turnover is not over £10.2 million
  • balance sheet total is not over £5.1 million
  • average number of employees is not over 50
