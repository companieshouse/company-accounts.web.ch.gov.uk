Criteria

Check your prepared accounts to see if you meet the criteria.

You cannot file full accounts using the new service if your company type is:

  • a charity
  • a PLC

You can file full accounts here if:

  • your directors' report includes details of no more than 20 directors
  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to
  • your balance sheet doesn’t have 'revaluation reserve'
  • you only need to add notes (if applicable) for 'accounting policies', 'employees', 'tangible assets', 'intangible assets', 'fixed assets investments', 'stocks', 'debtors', 'current assets investments', 'creditors - amounts falling due within one year', 'creditors - amounts falling due after one year', or 'off balance sheet arrangements'

Your company must also meet at least 2 of the following:

  • turnover is not over £10.2 million
  • balance sheet total is not over £5.1 million
  • average number of employees is not over 50
