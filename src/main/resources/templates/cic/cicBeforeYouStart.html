Use this service to file your annual CIC report and accounts with Companies House.

! Your filed CIC report and accounts will appear on the public record and online.

Before you start

You must get your accounts approved by your company directors before you file them.

It costs £15 to file. You can pay by MasterCard, Visa, Electron, Maestro or Delta debit or credit card.

Read guidance on what your annual accounts should include.

See a completed example of a CIC report.

You’ll need a Companies House password and authentication code to use this service - you’ll be able to register for these if you don’t have them.

The authentication code will be sent by post within 5 days. For security reasons, it can’t be sent by email or text.

Contact Companies House to get help filing your company’s accounts online.
